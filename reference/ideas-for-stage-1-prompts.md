# Ideas for the Stage 1 prompts #

This is a list of ideas for the Stage 1 prompts, in which President Donald Trump fires someone from the federal government for some reason, often absurd or ridiculous. The goal is to create prompts that are humorous, satirical, and in the spirit of the "You're Fired!" catchphrase from Trump's reality TV show "The Apprentice." These prompts can be used for various purposes, such as writing exercises, comedy sketches, or social media content.

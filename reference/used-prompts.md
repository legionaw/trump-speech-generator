# Prompts used for Trump Speech Generator #

# Prompt 1 #

Please generate a speech by president Donald Trump announcing the firing of the Secretary following a ridiculously absurd incident at the Pentagon, Washington, D.C., Earth.


Apparently, thanks to the Secretary who was dumb enough to fall for the email scheme, the Pentagon got scammed by a person who claimed to be a Nigerian prince living on a space colony. What a disaster.


He is now claiming it was an “accidental” misunderstanding. Trying to weasel out of his responsibilities, he claimed the President 'told' him to be more "aggressive" with making money. After all, the prince did promised him the financial gains, didn't he?


When that pathetic excuse failed, he, while making a sign of the Cross, then made this even more ridiculous claim that God "possessed" him and “made” him fall for the scam.


The President mocked the Secretary's name and his social skills, while bragging about his social skills. The Secretary is fired like a Mars-rover with a dead battery.


As a punishment, the ex-Secretary has been sent on a cold, uncomfortable, and slow transport, with bad food, out to an American planetary colony, which agreed to take him in, in the Alpha Centauri system. During his transit, he can think about what he did and how he embarrassed the country with his disastrous behavior. Oh, and he will have a computer aboard the ship that randomly tasers him throughout the long journey, just to teach him a lesson.

# Prompt 2 #

Please generate a speech by president Donald Trump announcing the firing of the Secretary and the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff following a ridiculously absurd incident at the Pentagon, Washington, D.C., Earth.


Apparently, the Secretary and the Chairman were discussing their plan to conspire with Carmen Sandiego and her V.I.L.E. organization to help them steal the Benin Bronzes from Nigeria.


Unfortunately, unbeknownst to both men, this totally illegal discussion was accidentally caught on live video recording by the nearby press corps at the Pentagon, for the public to see and hear. Apparently, the Secretary forgot to close the door to his office while this discussion was happening, leading to its public exposure, which was mocked by the President. Nigeria has summoned the American ambassador to demand an explanation. What a disaster.


Worst, they were caught openly admitting that they were making this plan *without* the President's knowledge. The President is really livid.


The men are now claiming it was an “accidental” misunderstanding. Trying to weasel out of their responsibilities, they blamed Carmen Sandiego, claiming she used her beauty and her charms to put them under her spell.


The President mocked both men's names and their communication skills, while bragging about his communication skills. Both the Secretary and the Chairman are fired like a Mars-rover with a dead battery.


As a punishment, both the ex-Secretary and the ex-Chairman have been sent on a cold, uncomfortable, and slow transport, with bad food, out to an American planetary colony, which agreed to take them in, in the Alpha Centauri system. During their transit, they can think about what they did and how they embarrassed the country with their disastrous behavior. Oh, and they will have a computer aboard the ship that automatically tasers them at random points throughout the long journey, just to teach them a lesson.


Meanwhile, Carmen Sandiego is still on the loose... But at least the conspiracy to steal the Benin Bronzes was foiled, thanks to the men's incompetence. Maybe the famed ACME Detective Agency will get her.

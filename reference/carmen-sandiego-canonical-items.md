## 3. Summary

- Your list is highly accurate for the major world landmarks featured in the games.
- For a truly exhaustive canonical list, consider adding: Rosetta Stone, Magna Carta, Declaration of Independence, Silver Pagoda, and Statue of Liberty's Torch.
- Some items in your list (Burj Khalifa, Great Mosque of Mecca) are not present in the original/classic games but may appear in modern remakes or as creative additions.
- The Sphinx's nose and Statue of Liberty's torch are sometimes treated as separate heists in the games.

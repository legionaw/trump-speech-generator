# Ideas for Stage 6 Prompts #

## Context ##
Stage 6 is for round-table panel discussions from Alpha Centauri. Here are some ideas for prompts that could be used for this stage:

1. The panel is discussing the recent announcement of a new space treaty with an alien civilization. The discussion becomes heated when one of the experts accuses the treaty of being a "total disaster" for humanity, leading to a fist-fight between him and another expert who defends the treaty as "the best deal we've ever made."
2. The panel is debating the ethics of using a new, powerful weapon developed by the Space Navy. One expert argues that it is necessary for national security, while another claims it is "lightweight" and will only lead to more conflict. The debate escalates into a fist-fight between the two experts.
3. The panel is discussing the recent discovery of a new alien species on a distant planet. One expert argues that they should be studied and protected, while another insists that they are a threat and should be eradicated. The disagreement leads to a fist-fight between the two experts.
4. The panel is debating the future of space colonization and whether it should be focused on expanding human presence or prioritizing environmental conservation. One expert advocates for aggressive colonization, while another emphasizes the importance of preserving alien ecosystems. The discussion becomes so heated that it results in a fist-fight between the two experts.
5. The panel is discussing the recent political scandal involving a high-ranking Space Navy official. One expert argues that the official should be held accountable and removed from their position, while another defends the official and claims that the scandal is being blown out of proportion. The disagreement leads to a fist-fight between the two experts.
6. The panel is debating the merits of a new space exploration initiative proposed by the President. One expert praises the initiative as "magnificent" and a step towards American greatness, while another criticizes it as "a total waste of resources." The debate escalates into a fist-fight between the two experts.
7. The panel is discussing the recent tensions with an alien civilization and whether diplomatic efforts should be intensified or if a more aggressive stance should be taken. One expert argues for diplomacy and cooperation, while another insists that a show of strength is necessary to deter aggression. The disagreement leads to a fist-fight between the two experts.
8. The panel is debating the implications of a recent technological breakthrough in starship propulsion. One expert claims that it will revolutionize space travel and open up new frontiers, while another argues that it is untested and could be dangerous. The discussion becomes so heated that it results in a fist-fight between the two experts.
9. The panel is discussing the recent discovery of a new habitable planet and whether it should be prioritized for colonization or preserved as a natural wonder. One expert advocates for colonization and resource exploitation, while another emphasizes the importance of conservation and scientific study. The disagreement leads to a fist-fight between the two experts.
10. The panel is debating the future of interstellar trade and whether it should be focused on expanding economic opportunities or ensuring fair trade practices. One expert argues for aggressive expansion and profit, while another emphasizes the importance of ethical trade and protecting vulnerable populations. The discussion becomes so heated that it results in a fist-fight between the two experts.

## Fist-Fight Scenarios ##

In each of the above scenarios, the fist-fight could be triggered by a particularly inflammatory statement or insult made by one of the experts. For example, in scenario 1, the expert who criticizes the treaty could call it "the worst deal in history," prompting the other expert to retaliate with an insult like "you're just a lightweight who doesn't understand diplomacy." This could escalate into a physical altercation between the two experts, requiring security to intervene with tasers to break up the fight.

### Different Types of Fist-Fights ###

The fist-fight could take various forms, such as a classic brawl where both experts throw punches at each other, or it could be more of a wrestling match where they grapple and try to overpower each other. The fight could also involve the use of nearby objects as weapons, such as chairs or microphones, which would add an element of chaos to the situation. The security team would need to quickly intervene to prevent any serious injuries and restore order to the panel discussion.



### Security Intervention ###

In the event of a fist-fight, the security team would quickly intervene to separate the two experts and restore order to the panel discussion. They would use tasers if necessary to subdue the individuals involved in the fight and prevent further escalation. The panel discussion would likely be paused or ended early as a result of the incident, and there may be consequences for the experts involved, such as being removed from future panels or facing disciplinary action.

#### Other potential ways for the security to intervene could include physically restraining the individuals involved in the fight, calling for backup if the situation escalates further, or using non-lethal crowd control measures to disperse any remaining tension in the room. The security team would prioritize the safety of all individuals present and work to de-escalate the situation as quickly and effectively as possible.

Phasers could also be used as a non-lethal means of subduing individuals involved in the fight, with settings that allow for temporary incapacitation without causing permanent harm. The security team would be trained in the use of such technology and would only deploy it as a last resort if other methods of intervention were unsuccessful.

Other weapons or tools that could be used by security to intervene in a fist-fight scenario might include stun grenades, pepper spray, or physical barriers to separate the individuals involved. The specific approach would depend on the severity of the altercation and the resources available to the security team at the time.

---These prompts are designed to create engaging and dramatic content for Stage 6, while also allowing for the incorporation of the unique setting and tone of the 2039 presidency. The inclusion of fist-fights adds an element of unpredictability and excitement to the panel discussions, while also providing opportunities for character development and conflict resolution.
